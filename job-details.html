Tutor Position

Company: Agazh Tech

Location: Bole, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Salary: 300 ETB / hr

Job Description:

We are seeking enthusiastic and experienced tutors to join our platform. Tutors will help students excel in various subjects, provide personalized learning, and support academic progress.

Responsibilities:

  • Offer tutoring in specific subjects as requested by students.
  • Ensure students understand key concepts and material.
  • Provide regular feedback to students and their families.
  • Support students in exam preparation and homework assistance.

Qualifications:

  • Experience in tutoring or teaching.
  • Strong communication skills.
  • Familiarity with different teaching methods and materials.
  • Patience and dedication to helping students succeed.
