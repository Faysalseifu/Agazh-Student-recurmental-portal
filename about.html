About Agazh

Agazh connects students and tutors across Addis Ababa, offering flexible recruitment and educational services for a better learning experience.

Why Choose Agazh?

Agazh bridges the gap between families and college students, offering a reliable and efficient platform for tutoring and recruitment services.

Flexible Scheduling

Choose times that suit both students and tutors for a seamless experience.

Trusted Matches

We use advanced algorithms to match tutors with students for optimal results.

Secure Payments

All transactions are handled securely, ensuring trust and reliability.

Multilingual Support

Our platform is available in English, Amharic, and Afan Oromo for better accessibility.
